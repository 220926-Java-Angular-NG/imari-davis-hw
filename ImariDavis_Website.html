About Me

As a graduate that majored in Digital Media for Game Design at the University of Central Florida, my primary goal is to gain experience for a career in game development as a software engineer. Since my high school years, I have aspired to join the gaming industry as a creator and bring my own ideas to life on a player’s console. I mostly lean toward technical aspects in game development such as programming which most of my experience is honed around, including implementing game mechanics and features as well as troubleshooting and repairing bugs. I am also interested in a lot of design aspects including but not limited to visuals and aesthetics, worldbuilding, and character design, which I hope to become more involved with in the future.


Another career goal that I am aspiring toward is being a visual artist, mostly as a means of furthering my career in game design if not something I do for my personal enjoyment and work. I have had quite an extensive history with visual art, from as far back as my childhood years, evolving from simple doodles to more complex works as time went on. My reason for creating art is to bring the works of my imagination to life on paper, creating visuals for stories or comics, and in the future, for video games, hence why I’ve set the goal for myself to practice whenever I can to continue improving. One of the things that I admire the most in game development is that it encourages many fields of expertise and creativity that I strive to be involved in.
